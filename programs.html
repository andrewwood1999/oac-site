Programs

School field trips, summer camps, and zero-waste action. Ocean literacy that sticks.

School Field Trips

Curriculum-linked shoreline learning, plastic pollution solutions, and optional paddleboarding for Grades 5–8. We bring BC curriculum to life on the beach.

Includes programs like Ocean Exploration, Ocean Camp, and Gumboots (names from current site).

Summer Camps

Adventure and stewardship all summer: TideRider, Sea to Sky, and Leadership Camp for Young Women (13–15). Paddling, shoreline ecology, confidence, leadership.

Zero Waste Initiatives

Workshops and community projects to reduce single-use plastics and marine debris — delivered with municipalities, school districts, and business areas.

This includes your Metro Vancouver / North Shore / York Region style projects.
