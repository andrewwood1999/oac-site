Our Programs

Place-based, ocean-first education for schools, youth, and communities along the coast.

School Field Trips

Curriculum-linked, hands-on shoreline experiences for Grades 5–7. Students learn about ocean ecosystems, plastic pollution, and solutions while exploring local beaches and (optionally) trying stand-up paddleboarding.

Includes programs like Ocean Exploration, Ocean Camp, and Gumboots. Content is aligned with BC curriculum.

Summer Camps

Week-long camps that combine ocean adventure and stewardship. Youth explore coastal ecosystems, paddle, play, and learn concrete ways to reduce their impact on the ocean.

Options for a range of ages, including leadership-focused programs for teens.

Zero Waste Initiatives

Workshops and community projects that support schools, municipalities, and businesses in reducing single-use plastics and marine debris.

Delivered in partnership with local governments and community organizations.
