About Us

Ocean Ambassadors Canada is a not-for-profit connecting people to the ocean and empowering them to protect it.

We believe that if you love the ocean, you’ll fight for it.

We deliver hands-on ocean literacy, plastic pollution education, and zero-waste action in coastal communities. Our work happens on beaches, docks, and paddleboards — not just in classrooms.

Patronage

We are honoured to have the Distinguished Patronage of the Lieutenant Governor of British Columbia.

Team & Governance

Our Founders, Board of Directors, program leaders, and Honorary Ambassadors are united by a commitment to ocean health, youth leadership, and community impact.

Status

Ocean Ambassadors Canada is a registered Canadian not-for-profit. Donations and sponsorships directly support programming, access, and ocean stewardship.
