About Us

We connect people with the ocean through hands-on experiences that inspire stewardship.

If you fall in love with the ocean, you’ll fight to protect it.

Ocean Ambassadors Canada is a not-for-profit organization based in British Columbia. We use beaches, docks, paddleboards, and coastal ecosystems as our classroom to teach ocean literacy, plastic pollution solutions, and zero waste habits.

Patronage

Honoured to receive the Distinguished Patronage of the Lieutenant Governor of British Columbia, recognizing our commitment to youth, education, and ocean health.

Team & Governance

Guided by founders, a volunteer Board of Directors, educators, and honorary ambassadors who bring expertise in paddling, education, conservation, and community engagement.

Not-for-Profit

Ocean Ambassadors Canada is a registered Canadian not-for-profit. Donations and sponsorships directly support programming and access.
